# Camail - Your AI Workspace Assistant

Welcome to **Camail**, an intelligent AI assistant designed to help you manage your Google Workspace, read and respond to emails, and schedule calendar events using simple natural language commands.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Smart Email Management**: Ask the agent to read unread emails, write drafts, reply to threads, and categorize your inbox.
- **Calendar Scheduling**: Check your availability, book meetings, invite attendees, and reorganize your weekly events.
- **Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)**: Connect your own provider keys (Google Gemini, OpenAI GPT, or Anthropic Claude) to power the AI.
- **Custom Templates & Instructions**: Set global instructions (e.g., writing tone, custom signatures, email layouts) that the agent will always follow.
- **Voice Control**: Click the microphone icon to dictate tasks to your agent hands-free.
- **Full Privacy Control**: Your API keys are saved strictly in your browser's local storage—never on our servers.

---

## 🚀 How to Get Started

### Step 1: Sign In

Click the **Sign In with Google** button in the sidebar. This allows Camail to securely cache your messages and calendar events.

### Step 2: Set Up Your AI Model

1. Go to the **Settings** page from the sidebar.
2. Select your preferred **AI Agent Model** from the dropdown menu (e.g., Gemini 2.5 Flash, GPT-5, or Claude Sonnet).
3. Paste the matching **API Key** in the credentials section.
   - _Tip: Custom API keys are saved locally in your browser cache for maximum security._

### Step 3: Add Custom Instructions

On the **Settings** page, write custom formatting instructions or email templates in the text area. Examples:

- _"Always write emails in a professional, brief, and polite tone. Sign off with 'Best regards, Kush'."_
- _"When writing meeting follow-ups, list the action items as bullet points."_

### Step 4: Start Chatting!

Go back to the **Home** page and type commands in the chat bar. Try these prompts:

- 💬 _"Read my last 3 unread emails and summarize them."_
- 💬 _"Do I have any meetings tomorrow?"_
- 💬 _"Draft an email to team@company.com about the project timeline update."_
- 💬 _"Schedule a 30-minute meeting with John tomorrow at 2 PM for code review."_

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy

- **OAuth 2.0 Encryption**: Your Google connection token is encrypted in our database using standard industry encryption wrappers.
- **Local Keys**: Your API keys never leave your browser; requests are sent securely directly to Google, OpenAI, or Anthropic endpoints.
- **Google Compliance**: Camail adheres strictly to Google APIs Limited Use Requirements. Your email and calendar data are never used to train AI models or shared with third parties.
